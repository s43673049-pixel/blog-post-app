About Web Trio

Web Trio is a learning-focused blog built for beginners and aspiring frontend developers.

What You'll Learn Here

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals
  • Real-world projects and layouts
  • Best practices and common mistakes

Why This Blog Exists

Most tutorials explain *what* to do, but not *why*. This blog focuses on clarity, reasoning, and practical understanding.

Who Is This For?

If you're learning web development and want explanations that respect your intelligence without assuming prior knowledge, you're in the right place.
